Our Services

Technology Programs

We host hackathons and provide coding classes in web design and app development to help engage dancers who may not have had any experience in these fields

Performances

We offer a wide variety of Dance classes for technologists to help create a bridge between these two sectors. We host performances at local community centers and other organizations to showcase the dances created by participants in our programs and allow us to engage with new learners who are interested in performance or technology.

Networking Opportunities

Participants in our program will be given the opportunity to take tours of performances agencies, theaters and major tech companies and meet with employees and CEO of these major corporations

Our Partners

NYCDOE

The New York Department of Education provides us with revenue for our programs and connects high schools around New York City to our programs.

BAM

The Brooklyn Academy of Music has been a major sponsor of our company since its creation. BAM provides us with rental space to host all of our dance classes and programs.

Lincoln Center

Lincoln Center is one of our major performance venues. Every year our fall and spring shows are featured on the Lincoln Center website and open to the general public to attend.

Google

The Google office in Manhattan provides us with office space and resources to host our coding programs. Participants in our program are also able to participate in all of google's annual hackathons.

General Assembly

General Assembly provides space for us to host our tech programs and fall hackathons.

360i

360i sponsors and helps to organize our spring hackathons and several of our technology programs.
